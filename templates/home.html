How a match works

Step 1

Create or join

One player creates a lobby and shares the game code. Up to four players join from any browser.

Step 2

Roll & move

On your turn, roll the dice, move your token, and trigger the tile effect: questions, traps, duels, and more.

Step 3

Answer & outplay

Answer blitz questions to gain coins, buy support cards, heal up, or push opponents into danger.

Step 4

Reach the goal

Be the first to reach the finish tile, or survive while others run out of HP — depending on the game mode.
